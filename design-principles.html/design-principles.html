White Space and Clean Design

Google has an exceptional design that reveals the genius behind their company. One of the most important details is the white space. There is a lot of white space here on this page, and it's not for nothing. The point was most likely to emphasize the company name and then direct the reader's attention to the search bar.

Fitt's Law

This same google image is perfect for Fitt's law, which is all about human interaction with computers. The way they control what the human sees has to do with all the color difference, proximity, and even white space. All of this combined makes what is called Fitt's Law
